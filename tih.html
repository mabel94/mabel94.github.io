The so-called "latte"

January 26, 2016

Fast food coffee shops such as Dunkin Donuts, Tim Hortons, and Starbucks have massacred the deliciousness of the latte and cappuccino. As warm beverages of all types are a source of comfort in my life, I have become somewhat elitist regarding their quality. To wit, I now believe that "latte" should not mean "heated milk with coffee-flavored syrup dissolved in a cup of sugar."
