Concentrated Coffee

February 16, 2016

The coffee I drink is getting increasingly more concentrated and bitter. Yesterday, I switched it up and got a latte from my new haunt, Manndibles in Mann library, but it just didn't quite approach the delicious bitterness of the cappuccino I usually get.

I'm also on to something. Today I had a macchiato, and I'm not talking about the Dunkin Donuts Caramel "macchiato". I mean a REAL macchiato. It was glorified espresso with a tiny spurt of foamed milk on the top to mitigate the ungodly bitterness. I got this instead of my usual cappuccino because it is a dollar less. Very refined. But anyway. It turns out, if you eat dark chocolate with macchiato, you get the full taste of the chocolate, the full taste of the espresso, and none of the piercing bitterness. I've never tried to sweeten my espresso drinks, but this is a great way of ensuring I can actually manage to finish the tiny cup without adulterating it with processed sugar or flavorful honey. Also, it's an excuse to eat a bar of chocolate at 11 am.
